Her Knowledge

I think I have the basics of HTML and CSS down, which is what I feel Web Design I is all about, familiarity with the languages. However, I have no idea how to make a website look appealing. I feel like my websites look straight out of 2004. I have syntax and structure, but no design flow. I definetly need help with positioning, grids, and content boxes. I think my strengths are persistance and resourcefulness. I will usually search for how to do something for hours before giving up or finding a work around, and I am very good at using search engines to find a "how to".
