♥ Gamer Girl ♥

I love board games. I've been playing them since before I could talk. I would play by myself or with family. Some of my favourites growing up were Don't Spill the Beans, Trouble, Sorry!, Monopoly, Chutes and Ladders, Uno, Janga, and My Little Pony Adventure. As an adult I've grown out of the classics and became familiar with the universe of fantasy. I go to conventions, roleplay, paint miniatures to game with, and spend whole weekends playing games.
